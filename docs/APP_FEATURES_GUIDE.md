# Keystone Treasury OS: Features & Capabilities

Keystone is not just a wallet or a dashboard; it is a **Sovereign Operating System** for digital assets. Designed for funds, DAOs, and high-net-worth individuals, Keystone transforms complex blockchain interactions into a streamlined, collaborative, and intelligent experience.

---

## 1. The Keystone Engine: The Agentic Soul
At the heart of Keystone is a multi-agent orchestration layer that coordinates complex workflows. Instead of manual clicking, the Engine uses specialized AI agents to execute your intent.
- **Autonomous Coordination**: 5 specialized agents (Lookup, Builder, Analysis, Transaction, Coordinator) working in tandem.
- **Smart Retries & Recovery**: The engine detects network congestion or failures and automatically suggests recovery paths.
- **Deterministic Execution**: Combines the reasoning of LLMs (Groq/OpenAI) with deterministic blockchain logic for 100% reliability.

## 2. The Command Layer: Natural Language Control
Experience the "Bloomberg for Blockchain." The Command Layer allows you to govern your entire treasury through a high-fidelity, real-time tactical interface.
- **Conversational Ops**: "Pay the dev team 500 USDC each" or "Rebalance 20% of SOL into USDC if price hits $150."
- **Tactical Feed**: A real-time stream of system directives, signature requests, and infrastructure syncs.
- **Universal Search**: Instantly find any transaction, asset, or protocol across your entire history.

## 3. Foresight Analytics: Visualizing the Future
Keystone doesn't just show you where you are; it shows you where you are going. Our analytics suite is built with **Predictive Intelligence**.
- **Predictive Runway**: Visualize your treasury's lifespan based on current burn, planned hires, and projected market shifts.
- **"What-If" Simulations**: Run simulations on vesting schedules, staking yields, or rebalancing outcomes before committing a single cent.
- **Market Sentiment Forecasting**: Integrated sentiment analysis that maps social and market trends directly onto your portfolio views.
- **Risk Radar**: A 360-degree sector scan that identifies concentration risks, volatility spikes, and OpSec vulnerabilities in real-time.

## 4. The Treasury Hub: Institutional Operations
A mission control for your assets, built for high-scale operations.
- **Operations Nexus**: Execute bulk payouts and multi-destination transfers in a single atomic transaction.
- **Yield Lab**: Compare and deploy into the highest-performing vault positions across the Solana ecosystem.
- **Governance Suite**: Native multisig support (Squads) with professional approval workflows and signature tracking.

## 5. Solana Atlas: Forensic Intelligence
The ultimate tool for market scouts and security-conscious treasurers.
- **Airdrop Scout**: Automatically discover and track eligibility for upcoming ecosystem rewards.
- **Scam & Rug Detection**: Real-time forensic screening of new tokens and protocols.
- **Whale Watcher**: Track the movements of institutional players and top-performing "smart money" wallets.

## 6. Keystone Studio & Marketplace
Custom-build your own treasury experience or extend it with community-built tools.
- **Keystone Studio**: A low-code environment to build custom dashboards, payment streams, and automation scripts.
- **Protocol Marketplace**: Browsable "App Store" for protocol-specific plugins (e.g., Jupiter swap widgets, Kamino vaults).
- **Embedded IDE**: Professional Monaco-based code editor for power users to tweak system logic on the fly.

## 7. Collaborative Multiplayer
Treasury management is a team sport. Keystone is built for real-time collaboration.
- **Live Presence**: See where your teammates are and what they are working on with live cursors and presence indicators.
- **Shared Context**: Collaborative planning boards where teams can draft strategies together before execution.
- **Permissioned Access**: Granular RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) to ensure the right people have the right level of control.
