## Part 5: Simulation Firewall Wording (for other decks/slides)

- **Avoid:** “Simulation Firewall mathematically verifies” (unless you’re ready to defend “mathematically” in diligence).
- **Use:** “Simulates the transaction on a mainnet fork and verifies expected outcomes before you sign.”

Your `simulation-firewall.ts` uses RPC `simulateTransaction` and returns balance changes + human summary; “verifies expected outcomes” is accurate. If you later use Helius shadow fork explicitly, you can say “mainnet fork” with no change to this line.
